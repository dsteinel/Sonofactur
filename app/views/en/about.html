SONOFACTUR is a music production studio founded by Ferdinand Breil and is located in Berlin. With a love detail music is being composed and produced here.

SONOFACTUR offers a broad scope of styles and genres, such as lyric piano music, fat electronic beats, indie-pop songs, rousing orchestrations and challenging avantgarde sounds. A flexible pool of musicians ensures that we always find the instruments that bring the desired genre to life.
