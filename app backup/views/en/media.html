• Best auf Ausbildung | Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Energy

    Informative and elaborately crafted at the same time: information film 'Mikrotechnologie' of the series Best of Ausbildung was produced by Birte Buß for the Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Energy. A fresh beat from SONOFACTUR is providing the right pace.

  • Sómente | Teatro Só

    A poetic street theatre piece by the group Teatro Só. The music is designed to let the people on the street dive deep into the atmosphere of the piece.

  • Der Ewige Tag | Alternative Score

    For this sad but wonderful short I had the chance to create an alternative score. The new soundtrack is reminiscient of the style of a silent movie score. Following the plot with great detail. This didn’t require any sound design.

  • ARK | SoundTrack_Cologne Contest

    For the SoundTrack_Cologne filmmusic contest, the participants were provided with a short film stripped of sound in order to create a new soundtrack for it. The visuals of this amazing animation required music that could equal their grandness and the orchestral sound provided just that. This version made it to the Shortlist. Sound design - Daniel Leseman

  • Headhunters | Free Composition

    A dramatic piece with a lot of suspense created by haunting strings and piano and energetic drums.

  • Der Koffer steht bereit | Free Composition

    In this piece a light yet melancholic piano part is supported by an arrangement of strings that creates a truly cinematic atmosphere.

  • 366 days | Free Composition

    A raw and edgy synth bass on a pumping beat. Glitchy synths and string samples. And, as a contrast, a lyric viola on top of all of this.

  • Dormir et Oublier | Free Composition

    Introduced by an atmospheric soundscape a hypnotic and slightly melancholic piano starts to unfurl.
